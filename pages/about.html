A platform for all your cricketing needs!

We started our journey for our love for cricket and wish to bring it to a wider audience around the globe. With Criconet, we provide one platform and ecosystem for all of your cricketing needs; we have an e-Coaching system with renowned coaches to guide you and help you excel at the game, and you can also have access to a community of like-minded individuals who have the same passion for cricket that you do.

We are an out-and-out digital platform providing e-Coaching, academy-led coaching, live streaming of local cricket matches, and social networking among aspiring cricketers, cricket enthusiasts, and experts.

One of the fastest growing Cricket platforms in India, We are also the only platform that provides live 1:1 e-Coaching to budding cricket talents from across the globe. With our dedicated coaching lessons, we want to bring out the full potential of cricket players who have the passion and talent to take them to new heights.

With the help of our robust ecosystem, We are able to provide uninterrupted coaching to all aspiring cricketers. We are an online platform where on-ground training is complemented with e-Coaching by accredited and expert coaches.

We also bring to our audience live streaming of the best of club leagues and other local level matches through its patent-pending technology right from the ground.

We offer real cricket fans and budding cricketers like you a platform to socialize with other fans and experts on current topics related to the game. We are the only platform that allows fans to give expert comments on the game, share knowledge, give their opinion, ask questions, upload pictures, and initiate discussions where other fans and experts can also participate.

Our Founder

Mr. Veer Sagar

Former budding cricket talent, who couldn t fulfill his dream to make it big because of an accident, Mr. Veer Sagar is a Cricket expert and enthusiast. Today, at an age of 80 years, he understands the struggle of a young cricketer to find a qualified coach and get proper training for himself.

While practicing with not yet discovered Sachin Tendulkar on nets, Mr. Sagar could realize the amount of talent the country had with minimum or no access to good coaches and finances. It became his dream to develop an ecosystem that would make accredited coaches and academies accessible to these talents.

With Criconet, Mr. Sagar could aspire to create a complete Cricket online platform that is interactive and can bring the Cricket fans, players, and coaches together to achieve one common goal which is Cricket.

Mission
