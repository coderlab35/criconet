Academy-led Hybrid Coaching

Our academy led hybrid coaching model connects local talent to the top cricket academies. An ever growing number of verified academies have partnered with us to offer an integrated online and on ground coaching module to give the best of both worlds to players like you.

Coaches continue to meet, assess and coach students in person while also delivering personalized training programs and support online. You can search and register with these academies conveniently from your device on our platform.
